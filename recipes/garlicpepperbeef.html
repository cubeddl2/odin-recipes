Garlic Pepper Beef(Inspired by Jollibee)

Garlic Pepper Beef is a dish from jollibee made with thinly sliced beef, garlic bits, and gravy to top it off. It is a childhood favorite for many Filipinos. Unfortunately, it has been phased out. This recipe is a homemade version of this dish so that people craving it can make it right at home.

Ingredients

Serves 2-4 people
  • 1 pound thinly-sliced beef
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper

For the gravy:

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 tsp garlic power
  • 1/2 Knorr beef cube
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper powder

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, combine beef with soy sauce then massage the meat while ensuring that each side of the meat is in contact with the sauce. Heat oil in a pan and stir fry the beef in medium heat until it turns brown. Transfer into a serving plate.
  2. To make the gravy, melt the butter in a pan in medium heat and add the flour. Whisk continously until it turns into a roux or a paste. Add beef cube and water then continue to whisk.
  3. Season with garlic powder, salt and pepper and continue to whisk and cook until it turns into a thick gravy. Add more water if the gravy becomes too thick
  4. Add the beef in the pan and stir. Add fried garlic then serve with steamed rice.
  5. Enjoy your homemade garlic pepper beef!
